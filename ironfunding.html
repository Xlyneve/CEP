Iron Funding Checker

Created by Xlyn

Quick screening tool for the updated aligned POAC + Pharmac Ferinject pathway in Metro Auckland. This is a helper only — always confirm against the current POAC form, Pharmac Special Authority, and clinic policy.

1. Patient + bloods

CRP is required when ferritin is above 20 and up to 50 mcg/L.

Hb can be entered for documentation, but funding logic is based on documented diagnosis of anaemia.

2. Updated aligned criteria

The patient must have diagnosed anaemia and meet at least one qualifying pathway:

Ferritin ≤20 mcg/L
Ferritin >20–50 mcg/L and CRP ≥5 mg/L
Chronic inflammatory disease with symptoms of anaemia

The patient must also meet at least one additional criterion:

Updated pathway: patient has diagnosed anaemia + one qualifying iron pathway + one additional criterion.
